### Consumer Complaint Database Complaint Explorer
[https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-complaints/search/?chartType=line&dateInterval=Month&dateRange=3y&date_received_max=2026-03-25&date_received_min=2023-03-25&lens=Product&searchField=all&subLens=sub_product&tab=Trends](https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-complaints/search/?chartType=line&dateInterval=Month&dateRange=3y&date_received_max=2026-03-25&date_received_min=2023-03-25&lens=Product&searchField=all&subLens=sub_product&tab=Trends)

From 2021 to 2024 I worked as UI/UX designer and developer as a subcontractor at the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, where I worked on a team supporting the Consumer Complaint Database Complaint Explorer. Complaint Explorer is an application used to analyze the CCDB data, allowing users to explore trends and patterns, filter data by various criteria, search through the database, and view specific complaints.

My role was primarily to provide front-end development support, initially by assisting in refactoring the application from Angular.js to React and Redux, and to act in a UX design capacity as needed. As such, I collaborated with bureau experts and stakeholders outside the team to design new features, develop prototypes, demonstrate those prototypes to decision-makers, and ultimately implement those features.
